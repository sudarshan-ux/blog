1. Indian Institute of Management Calcutta (IIMC)

  • Background: Established in 1961, IIMC is the oldest IIM in the country and a pioneer in management education. It was set up with the vision of developing leaders and managers who could navigate the complexities of a rapidly changing business landscape.
  • Unique Features:
    • IIMC's illustrious faculty, global collaborations, and emphasis on research and innovation set it apart.
    • The institute's commitment to academic excellence has led to consistent recognition as one of the premier business schools in India.
    • IIM Calcutta's flagship Post Graduate Program in Management (PGP) is a two-year residential program that covers a broad spectrum of management disciplines. The institute also offers executive programs such as PGPEX, PGPEX-VLM, and PGDBA.
  • Placement Success Rate: IIM Calcutta boasts a remarkable 100% placement success rate, underlining the effectiveness of its programs in preparing students for the corporate world.
  • Average CTC: Graduates from IIMC command an average CTC of approximately INR 28 LPA, reflecting the high value placed on their skills by top recruiters.
  • Top Recruiters: The institute's strong industry connections attract leading recruiters, including Amazon, McKinsey, Goldman Sachs, and Tata Consultancy Services (TCS).

2.Globsyn Business School:

  • Background: Established in 2002, Globsyn Business School has been a dynamic player in the management education sector, leveraging technology and innovation to provide a unique learning experience.
  • Unique Features:
    • Globsyn is known for its industry-driven curriculum, emphasis on entrepreneurship, and the use of cutting-edge technology in education.
    • The institute has gained recognition for its incubation centre, supporting student-led startups.
  • Placement Success Rate:The institute maintains a commendable 90% placement success rate, showcasing the practical relevance of its programs in the corporate world.
  • Average CTC: Graduates from Globsyn Business School achieve an average CTC of around INR 6 LPA, reflecting the industry-relevant skills instilled during their programs.
  • Top Recruiters: Companies such as Capgemini, Cognizant, Genpact, and Axis Bank regularly recruit from Globsyn Business School.

3. Heritage Business School:

  • Background: Established in 2003, Heritage Business School is affiliated with the Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology. The institute was founded with the vision of creating a global talent pool through quality education.
  • Unique Features:
    • Heritage Business School distinguishes itself through its industry-oriented education, collaborative research initiatives, and a holistic approach to skill development.
    • The institute fosters a vibrant campus life and a diverse student community.
  • Placement Success Rate: The institute maintains a strong placement record, with a success rate of 92%, showcasing its commitment to bridging the gap between academia and industry
  • Average CTC: Graduates from Heritage Business School achieve an average CTC of approximately INR 5.5 LPA, reflecting the practical skills and knowledge imparted during their programs.
  • Top Recruiters: Companies like Wipro, Infosys, Axis Bank, and Asian Paints regularly recruit from Heritage Business School.

4. Army Institute of Management (AIM):

  • Background: Established in 1997 by the Army Welfare Education Society, AIM Kolkata was founded to impart quality education to the wards of Army personnel. The institute embodies the discipline and commitment of the Indian Army.
  • Unique Features:
    • AIM Kolkata stands out for its disciplined academic environment and a curriculum designed to instil leadership qualities.
    • The institute maintains strong ties with the corporate world to ensure industry relevance in its programs.
  • Placement Success Rate: AIM Kolkata maintains a commendable 93% placement success rate, reflecting the adaptability and skills of its graduates.
  • Average CTC: Graduates from AIM Kolkata secure an average CTC of around INR 7 LPA, underlining the institute's focus on producing industry-ready professionals.
  • Top Recruiters: The institute has strong ties with recruiters such as the Indian Army, ICICI Bank, HDFC Bank, and Deloitte.
